About Me


During my first year in college I had a tough time figuring out what it was I wanted to do when I grew up. I started my very first coding lesson over the summer in July of 2017 on Free Code Camp and immediately realized that this is what I want to do. Changing my education path to a coding bootcamp was one of the biggest and scariest decisions I have ever made yet I truly believe it was the right decision.

Currently I'm in a bootcamp designed for full stack web development and I have also dabbled in Ruby a little. Currently considering getting into Python soon.

I flourish in small groups and enjoy learning from others. All my life I have been taught to lead by example and it has become part of who I am.
